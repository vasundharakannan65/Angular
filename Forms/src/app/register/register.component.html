Steps to fill the form

In order to fill up Online application form in Railways candidates need to visit the official website i.e. www.indianrailways.gov.in.

  • After reaching homepage candidates need to select and hit on appropriate zone link
  • A new homepage of that chosen railway zone will appear in front of you
  • Select and hit on recruitment tab available at that new homepage
  • From the next screen choose the notification link and then hit on apply online tab
  • Now if you are a new user then hit on Registration tab

REGISTRATION:
  • Fill up the online registration form and complete all the details
  • Check out your mail/Phone number and note down the registration ID
